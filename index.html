Why I built this website

I started learning piano thanks to Charles at Cornell Music Academy and his courses "An intro to the Piano". Building something for the comunity is a way contribute back somehow, or at least I hope so.

I started doing all exercises that he was providing. Those exercises were really good but I think that they are meant to be a starting exercise. This to understand the basics to work your way up and strengthen your skills. To become a second nature.

Charles is an amazing teacher, he can explain in a simple way difficult topics and make them stick.

If you are interested in learning piano I highly recommend to take a look at his courses at Cornell Music Academy. Same applies for Music theory, Harmony and Improv.

If you are not sure that will like the way he teach maybe you can start watching some of his video on Youtube and who knows maybe you will become a pianist too as I am doing.
